About Me

I'm a Senior Analyst at Headlands Capital working within the Deal Team, specializing in Private Equity Secondaries. I build comprehensive financial models, conduct sector-wise research, and create analytical content that drives investment decisions.

My role involves preparing detailed one-pagers and Investment Committee (IC) memos that facilitate smooth deal approvals with senior leadership including Principals and Partners. With expertise in DCF models, comparable company analysis, and industry research, I help stakeholders make informed investment decisions.

My work combines quantitative analysis with qualitative insights, providing actionable recommendations backed by thorough research and robust financial modeling.
